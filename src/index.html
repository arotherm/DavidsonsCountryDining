Our Story

Who We Are

Located near Innisfil’s Friday Harbour Resort, Davidson’s Country Dining is a cozy log cabin restaurant owned by Dan Davidson since 1984. With its rustic charm and eclectic decor, our eatery offers a unique dining experience featuring a menu that ranges from hearty cottage fare to fine dining, all made from scratch. Discover why we’re a beloved gem in Innisfil, complete with a resident ghost named Earl who adds a touch of mystery and charm. From prime rib to homemade desserts, our diverse menu and welcoming ambiance ensure a memorable dining experience.
