About Us

The Story of Davidson’s Country Dining

Nestled in the heart of Innisfil, Davidson’s Country Dining has been a beloved local treasure since its doors first opened in 1980. Born out of a passion for great food and a love for the community, Davidson’s offers more than just a meal; it offers a true culinary experience steeped in tradition and warmth.

Our story began when Dan, decided to create a place where the pace of life could slow down, and people could gather over hearty, home-cooked meals. What started as a small family operation has grown into a staple of the community, known for its cozy atmosphere, friendly service, and menu filled with locally-sourced ingredients.

"At Davidson’s, we believe that the best meals are the ones that bring people together. Our food is a celebration of the simple, honest ingredients that make every dish special." Dan Davidson Owner & Head Chef
